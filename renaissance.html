History

The Renaissance period flourished in Florence, Italy in the 14th century. It is a French word that means "rebirth". It is inevitable to talk about the Renaissance without the Medieval period, a dark time where Europe had a dramatic change socially, politically and economically. During the Medieval times,the economic class was basically the clergy and the peasants which were the poorest people. Right after the Medieval period ended in Europe, the Renaissane started to appear. It came with a new class, the middle class. The renaissance brought new ideas of painting which portrayed naturality. A Renaissance man was Michelangelo, one of his masterpiece renaissance works is David, a marble sculpture that shows the technique of classical humanism. The second of the great Renaissancess artists was Urbinoborn Raphael, one of his marvelous paintings was The Alba Madonna, which shows harmony, freshness, and talent. Raphael organized the composition according to simple geometric shapes : the triangle, the circle, and the trapezoid. The freedom of learning from the Greeks let artists such as Raphael and Michelangelo offer a different perspective on art.

Paintings

1495 to 1498. The Last Supper is a mural painting by the Italian Renaissance artist Leonardo da Vinci. The painting represents the scene of the Last Supper of Jesus with the Twelve Apostles.

1508 to 1512. The Creation of Adam is a fresco painting by Italian artist Michelangelo, which forms part of the Sistine Chapel's ceiling.
